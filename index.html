We are truckjunkie

At Truckjunkie it's all about passion - passion for trucks, design and quality. What once started in a shed grew into a globally recognized concept in the truck world. Truckjunkie developed into a place where truck enthusiasts from all over the world come together for their favorite truck accessories.

With an extensive range, we offer truck enthusiasts everything to make their truck shine. Whether you drive a Scania, DAF, Man, Renault, Volvo or another brand of truck, you will find what you are looking for at Truckjunkie.

Our products are not just accessories; they are expressions of style and personality. From interior upgrades to exterior lighting, from personalized gadgets to custom options - you'll find it all at Truckjunkie.

With a passionate team we strive for satisfied customers every day. That's why we offer fast ordering and delivery processes and the promise to solve unexpected problems together.

Truckjunkie is more than a webshop; it is a community of everyone who embraces the trucking lifestyle. So whether you are a true truck enthusiast looking for an item to show your passion for trucks, or as a trucker you want to make a powerful statement on the road, Truckjunkie is the right place for you.

Years of experience

At Truckjunkie we have been setting trends in the world of trucks and accessories for years. As seasoned truckers, we know what it's all about. Our experience ensures that we deliver sturdy products and top services that perfectly suit your life as a trucker or truck enthusiast. At Truckjunkie you will find a partner who shares your passion and helps you make your truck stand out with rugged, practical accessories.

Unique truck accessories

Enter a world full of possibilities to give your truck a personal touch at Truckjunkie. Here you will not only find custom-made license plates with a twist that suits only you, but also exclusive light boxes that bring your design to life. From pins and signs to flags that give your truck its own identity, our range is bursting with items that will make your truck stand out. But we go further than just style - our practical accessories such as mud flaps, dirt repellents and sun visors are not only functional, but also radiate character on the road. At Truckjunkie it's all about the unique truck experience.
